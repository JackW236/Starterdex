Welcome to the Starterdex

This website is dedicated to the 27 starter Pokémon from across all generations. Whether you're a seasoned trainer or new to the world of Pokémon, this site is here to help you learn about the unique characteristics and types of each starter Pokémon.

Explore the Pages:

  • Starterdex: A visual list of all 27 starter Pokémon with images and Pokédex entries.
  • Generations: See which generation each starter is from and how many were introduced.
  • Typing: Grow an understanding of the 3 types every starter Pokémon must be.
  • Video: Watch our weekly recommended Pokémon YouTube video!
  • Contact: A contact form to get in touch or leave feedback about the Starterdex project.
